What you're seeing

Drag the orange pin anywhere on the map. The page calls GET /api/v1/checkin on every drag and updates the live response. When you're within ~35 m of a library, the service detects the venue and, if you're within a section (~6 m), reports the zone and surfaces the reading list for that section.
